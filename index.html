Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kooluma?

Kooluma is an innovative language learning platform connecting users with local South African tutors for personalized language and cultural lessons. We aim to bridge cultures and make learning fun, accessible, and immersive.

Which languages can I learn on Kooluma?

You can learn popular South African languages like isiZulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans, and more, directly from local experts.

How does Kooluma work?

Simply sign up, choose your preferred language, and book sessions with vetted local tutors. Lessons are conducted virtually, providing convenience and safety while ensuring an interactive learning experience.

Who are the tutors?

Our tutors are South African locals, experienced in teaching language and culture. They bring authenticity and depth to your learning experience, sharing insights into their heritage and traditions.

Is Kooluma suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, Kooluma offers tailored lessons to suit all levels.

Can I customize my lessons?

Yes! You can personalize your learning experience by focusing on specific topics such as travel phrases, cultural insights, or conversational practice.

How much does it cost?

Pricing details will be announced soon! We’re committed to offering flexible and affordable options to match your learning goals and budget. Stay tuned for updates!

Is Kooluma only for tourists?

Not at all! While we cater to tourists seeking cultural immersion, Kooluma is perfect for anyone curious about South African languages—be it for personal, academic, or professional reasons.

Do I need any special software for the virtual lessons?

No special software is needed! Lessons take place in secure, interactive virtual meeting rooms accessible via your browser or device.

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple: • Sign up on our platform. • Select your language and tutor. • Book your first session and start learning!

What if I need to cancel or reschedule a session?

We understand plans change! You can cancel or reschedule a session up to 24 hours in advance without any penalty.

How can I become a tutor on Kooluma?

If you’re a South African local passionate about sharing your language and culture, we’d love to have you on board. Visit our "Become a Tutor" page to apply.

How can I contact Kooluma for support?

You can reach us via email at support@kooluma.com or through the contact form on our website. We’re here to help!
